Unis Dating App

Unis is a dating app that promotes healthy interaction with people. We accomplish this by ensuring members have minimal/no criminal history. Providing a safe environment for our members to meet likeminded singles is our top priority.

Features

Background Checks

Background checks are required for membership. Date knowing all users are vetted, and meet our minimum requirements to use the service.

Date Reservations

We promote getting out in the real world and dating. Built in features make it easy to book resturant reservations near both parties locations.

Video Speed Dating

Dating apps have never been so fun! Enter a round with 20 singles, chat for 45 seconds and swipe directly afterwards if you'd like a 2nd date!

Messaging

Limited messaging & video chat encourges users to get into the real world. Utlilize out features to make that initial connection!

Profiles

User profiles are not filled out by the user who created them. However, every date you go on is an oppurtunity to rate that person.

Community

Unis stands for united. We are united while dating meaning we have your back. This community has implimented satey measures to ensure your enjoyment and well-being!

How it works

1Sign up and go through the background check. This might take 24-48 hours. Keep an eye on your email, we will send you any information there to finish up the process.
2After your approval and your membership fee has cleared, you can enter into your first speed date round. Our rounds start at 8pm and end at 11pm EST. Clear your calanders and get ready to meet some interesting people.
3Getting matches & getting dates is as easy as 1-2-3. Once you match with a user you can have limited messaging 24/7 through our app. Make sure to talk about date ideas, our intergrations with OpenTable and Atom make it easier to book reservations for dinner and a movie. These reservations can link to your Google calander for easy reminders!
